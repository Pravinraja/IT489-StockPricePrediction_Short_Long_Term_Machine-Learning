Arima Stock Price Predictions

Explained

ARIMA is based on the principal of autocorrelation - correlation between "nearby" data points, up to some lag k.
When you make predictions, you are saying "Since tomorrow's data should be correlated with the previous k days, how can I leverage that correlation to decide what direction/value is most likely?
